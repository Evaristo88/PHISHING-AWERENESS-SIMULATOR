# Ethical and Legal Guidance

This tool is designed for controlled, authorized awareness training only.

## Do

- Obtain written authorization from leadership.
- Use whitelisted, internal recipient lists.
- Clearly label landing pages as training content.
- Share results with privacy and HR teams as needed.
- Focus on education, not punishment.

## Do Not

- Run campaigns against external parties.
- Collect real passwords or sensitive data.
- Hide the training intent indefinitely.
- Use the tool for harassment or coercion.

## Privacy Considerations

- Store reports in a secure location.
- Limit who can access the campaign results.
- Retain data only as long as needed for training.

## Accountability

You are responsible for ensuring your usage complies with company policy, local laws, and ethical standards.
